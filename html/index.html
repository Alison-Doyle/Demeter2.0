Sourcing our Clay

Amilie, the owner of Demeter takes great pride in its commitment to sustainability and connection with the local community. Inspired by the lush landscapes and rich heritage of Ireland, she sources clay locally to craft her exquisite plant pots. By utilizing clay from nearby regions, Demeter not only minimizes its environmental impact but also support local businesses and reduce transportation-related carbon emissions. This approach not only ensures the authenticity of the products but also fosters a sense of place and belonging in each meticulously handmade plant pot. Embracing the unique qualities of locally-sourced clay, our creations embody the essence of Irish craftsmanship, bringing a touch of the Emerald Isle's natural beauty to your home.
