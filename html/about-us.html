About Us

The Artist

Meet Amelia, the artisan behind Demeter, a haven for handcrafted plant pots in the heart of Sligo. Nestled amidst the rugged beauty of this enchanting town, Amelia draws inspiration from the rolling hills and serene landscapes that surround her. With a formal education in fine art and pottery from the National College of Art and Design (NCAD), her skills were further honed through an apprenticeship in England, where she imbibed diverse techniques and perspectives. Amelia's journey began with a passion for both art and nature, culminating in the establishment of Demeter, a small business that reflects her commitment to sustainability and aesthetics.

Each meticulously crafted piece not only showcases her technical expertise but also carries a touch of her Irish heritage. Each pot tells a story, echoing the timeless beauty of Sligo and inviting nature into homes around the world. Explore Demeter's collection and bring a piece of Sligo's charm, enriched by Amelia's artistic education and international experience, to your living space.
